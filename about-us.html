StellarStays Story

Namaste 🙏,

We didn't start with a fancy plan. We started by listening.

Long chats with homestay owners in mountain villages, coastal towns, and big cities all revealed the same problem: beautiful homes, warm hosts, and incredible stories held back by online chaos and platforms built for hotels, not for them.

After 17 years in technology and more than a decade leading tech product teams and running vacation rentals in the US, I returned to India and set out on countless road trips across the north and south, meeting hosts from Uttarakhand's hills to Kerala's backwaters. I saw firsthand how passion could turn simple spaces into stellar memories.
